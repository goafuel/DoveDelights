Our Culinary Story

Founded by master chef Maria Fernandes, Dove Caterers brings generations of Goan culinary tradition to your event. We specialize in:

  • Traditional Goan seafood dishes
  • Portuguese-inspired cuisine
  • Vegetarian Goan specialties
  • Authentic Goan desserts

Using only the freshest local ingredients and traditional cooking methods, we create unforgettable dining experiences for weddings, corporate events, and private parties throughout Goa.
