About Dove Nest

A serene getaway for your special occasions

Nestled in the beautiful landscapes of Goa, Dove Nest Farmhouse & Party Hall offers a perfect blend of rustic charm and modern amenities. Our venue is designed to host unforgettable weddings, parties, corporate events, and family gatherings.

With spacious indoor and outdoor areas, lush greenery, and top-notch facilities, we provide everything you need to create magical moments that last a lifetime.
